Not into AI? Let’s be friends & try something different.

January 2026 — Can you email me if any of this resonates with you?

Among all the thoughtful, well researched, brilliantly explained critiques of AI, I keep comng back to a simple aching thought:

Does the AI revolution actually make my friends and family happier?

I don’t see it. Or at least it’s a stark contrast to the joy of a 2005 iPod Shuffle.

Could something radically different from the dysfunction and depression of today’s tech actually… work? Even thrive? Become super popular and refreshing? A zig to a zag?

Imagine a parallel universe starting from that iPod Shuffle.
